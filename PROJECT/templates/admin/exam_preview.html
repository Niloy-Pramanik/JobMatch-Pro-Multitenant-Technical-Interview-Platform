What is the primary purpose of implementing a Kanban system in lean manufacturing environments?
A
To increase inventory levels for buffer stock management
B
To reduce machine setup times through automation
C
To visualize workflow and limit work-in-progress inventory
D
To eliminate all quality control checkpoints in production
Explanation
Kanban is a visual workflow management method that helps teams visualize their work, limit work-in-progress (WIP), and maximize efficiency. It uses visual signals (cards) to trigger action and control the flow of materials or information through a production system, which is a core principle of lean manufacturing.
